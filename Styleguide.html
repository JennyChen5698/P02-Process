About

Bobar provides exclusive bubble teas. With an organic base of green or black tea, our delicious drinks are described as a nudge along the evolutionary scale. Bobar uses Japanese-designed teaspresso machine that has tea brewing down to a science. Leaves encased in silk bags are prepared using a special tea blender to best optimize the flavour.
